Digital • Green • Inclusive | Built for Zambia

Why this matters

Up to 30% of fresh produce in Zambia never reaches the market. Smallholders face unreliable transport, fragmented demand, and poor cold-chain access—leading to post-harvest losses, lower incomes, and higher food prices.

Eval Logistics Network solves this by orchestrating demand, supply, and transport on one platform—so food moves faster, fresher, and fairer.

Our mission

Move food, not waste—so farmers earn more and communities eat better.

Our vision

A climate-smart, digitally connected food logistics grid across Zambia and the region.

The digital, climate-smart logistics platform

We match produce to buyers and vehicles in real time, optimize routes, and verify delivery—cutting emissions and post-harvest losses.

Smart matching

Farmers list produce; the platform matches to buyers and transporters based on freshness windows, routes, and price.

Live tracking

See produce move from farm to buyer. Transparency reduces disputes, delays, and losses.

Verified delivery

Proof-of-delivery and ratings build trust and repeat business for farmers and transporters.

Climate-smart by design: higher utilization, fewer empty miles, faster time-to-market, and cold-chain prioritization where it matters.

Scaling for national impact

We start in high-volume horticulture corridors, onboard regional hubs, and expand through partnerships with cooperatives, FMCGs, and transport unions.

  • Partnerships with co-ops, aggregators, and market councils
  • Digital payments and micro-insurance integrations
  • Cold-chain pilots in heat-risk districts
  • Regional expansion to SADC trade routes
